Sweet Treat Mod

Sep 21, 2014

Download latest version 1.7.10-1.0 (2014-11-23)
All versions

Introducing the Sweet Treat mod! Here you can find scrummy sweet things to sugar-charge your world. Some of them have interesting effects…

As gesture of welcome, there is a good chance of spawning a tower of chocolate blocks nearby when you enter the world.

New blocks

  • Chocolate
  • White chocolate
  • Marshmallow
  • Candyfloss
  • Shortbread

Chocolate blocks and White chocolate blocks spawn naturally in the world (White chocolate blocks are rarer). Chocolate blocks can also be created with a crafting recipe. Other blocks are currently only available through the creative menu: this mod isn’t finished yet.

New items

  • Chocolate bar: edible, restores hunger bar
  • White chocolate bar: edible even if you’re not hungry; (hint: you really should eat this!)
  • Mini marshmallow: gives a small jump boost for a short time

Crafting recipes

  • Chocolate block:

    cocoa bean, cocoa bean, cocoa bean

    cocoa bean, cocoa bean, cocoa bean

    cocoa bean, milk bucket, cocoa bean

  • Choocolate bar:

    Smelt one chocolate block to obtain chocolate bars (6).

  • White choocolate bar:

    Either:

    Smelt one white chocolate block to obtain white chocolate bars (6).

    Or:

    gold block, [empty], gold block

    chocolate bar, chocolate block, chocolate bar

    gold block, milk bucket, gold block

  • Mini marshmallows

    Craft with any marshmallow block (1)
